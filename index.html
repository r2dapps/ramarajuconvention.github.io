About Venue

Established in 2014, Ramaraju Convention Centre in Vijayawada Main Road, Machilipatnam. is one of the most sought after one banqueting facilities in the city. It was setup with a view to provide an elegant and superior banqueting space to cater to the varied requirements of their clients. Whatever the celebration may be, big or small, it is this firm's endeavour to make each celebration, a rousing success. It is one of the preferential venue partners to host corporate events as well as intimate gatherings and ceremonies like birthday bashes, mehendi, haldi, weddings or receptions. Occupying a favourable location Vijayawada Main Road, this grand banqueting facility is at a strategically advantageous spot. On the Vijayawada Main Road, it is at an unmistakable location. Get in touch with this banquet hall on the following numbers: +(91)-8672-223456. It is one of the best banquet halls in Vijayawada Main Road, Machilipatnam.

MAIN HALL

The central zero-pillar hall that’s acoustic friendly for musical concerts, and seminars alike. 2500 square feet of air-conditioned space with the ceiling a 16 feet high, The Hall boasts of state of the art IT infrastructure and design with glass front on 3 sides with a lake view and an LED wall on the fourth side.The hall can accommodate up to 3000 persons in theatre-style seating while ~1200 persons in round-table seating.

The Lawn

The lawns that span 55,000 square feet of open air space provide a breathtaking view of the lake. A separate entrance to these lawns makes it as convenient as you would want it to be.
